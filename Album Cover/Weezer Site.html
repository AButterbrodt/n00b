The Blue Album

Weezer

May 10 1994

In their debut self titled album Weezer, referred to primarily as The Blue Album, Weezer achieved mainstream success with three singles: "Undone-The Sweater Song", "Buddy Holly", and "Say it Ain't So", and music videos produced by Spike Jonze.
